HELIUM

The Helium project is a personal UI design exercise focused on the creation of a playlist application inspired by Deezer, Spotify and others. The aim was to learn and practice UI/UX design, with an emphasis on the Dark theme and the use of gradients, shadows and margins. The colors chosen are warm and discreet to offer users a comfortable and efficient experience, with particular attention paid to each button and space to guarantee an excellent user experience.

BANK'

Bank'App is a mobile banking interface design project completed in a weekend. It features a user-friendly design with a Dark theme and colorful cards to highlight essential information, such as spending, spending categories and account selection. The aim was to rethink the overall design in two days, which enriched the thinking behind the layout of the elements.

Kronos Website

Kronos is a project combining UI design and product design, focused on the complete design of a chronometer. The aim was to create a modern, old-school design, with the emphasis on raw metal buttons and an LED display. After a phase of global reflection, the stopwatch was modeled using CAD software, and its website was designed with a raw, elegant style, highlighting the product through dynamic renderings. The graphic identity, marked by bright green, recalls the fast, sporty side of the product.
